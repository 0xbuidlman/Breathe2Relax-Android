Before Starting Breathing Session

This breathing exercise is safe for almost everybody. But if you experience any unpleasant sensations, if you have difficulty breathing or feeling dizzy, nervous, or out of control, just go back to breathing normally. Remember that the purpose of this exercise is to help you learn how to slow down and relax. You can shorten or lengthen the time of each breath to make it more comfortable for you. Now get into a comfortable position in a quiet place that is free from distraction. Press start when you are ready to begin the guided exercise.
