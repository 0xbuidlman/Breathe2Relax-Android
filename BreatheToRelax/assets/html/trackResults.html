Track Results

As you breathe to relax you can set how much stress you feel before and after the breathing exercise and over time you should see how the breathing is helping you reduce stress.

These graphs display your stress ratings before and after your breathing practice. Try to practice every day.

Legend

The vertical axis of this graph is the recorded stress level (from 0-100; 0=relaxed, 100=stressed).
The horizontal axis represents the day of the selected month.

Operation

The graph shows a single month at a time. To change months:

  • Swipe the screen left or right to go back or forward a month.
  • Press the left or right buttons to go back or forward a month.
  • Long-press the left of right buttons to go back or forward a year.
