Breathe2Relax and T2

Breathe2Relax

Diaphragmatic breathing has widely known benefits in reduction of stress, tension and anxiety - calming the body’s “fight or flight” response to better focus the mind and the body. The Breathe2Relax application is a portable skill rehearsal tool for practicing diaphragmatic breathing. It can be used as a standalone stress reducer or augment face-to-face care with a healthcare professional. Due to its portability, this guided exercise is easily accessible when it is needed most.

The National Center for Telehealth and Technology|T2|

researches, develops, evaluates and deploys new and existing technologies for Psychological Health (PH) and Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) across the Department of Defense (DoD). T2 is a component center of the Defense Centers of Excellence for Psychological Health and Traumatic Brain Injury (DCoE), which leads a collaborative global network to promote the resilience, recovery and reintegration of Warriors and their families who face psychological health and traumatic brain injury issues.
